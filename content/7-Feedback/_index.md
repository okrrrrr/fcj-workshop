This section contains my personal feedback about my experience participating in the First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ) program at AWS Vietnam. I hope my sharing will help the FCAJ team understand what worked well and identify areas for improvement.

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The FCAJ community provides a very supportive and collaborative learning environment. The online workspace through Discord and Notion is well-organized, making it easy to access learning materials and communicate with peers. The weekly worklog system helps maintain discipline and creates a sense of accountability. I particularly appreciate the openness of the community where everyone is encouraged to ask questions and share their learning journey.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentors demonstrate exceptional dedication in guiding participants through complex cloud concepts. They provide detailed explanations, encourage self-discovery through hands-on labs, and are always available to clarify doubts. The administrative team maintains smooth communication channels and ensures all participants have access to necessary resources. What stands out is the mentor's philosophy of guiding rather than giving direct answers, which fosters genuine learning.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
As a Computer Science student, the FCAJ program perfectly bridges theoretical knowledge with practical cloud computing skills. The curriculum covers AWS services that are directly applicable to real-world software development and system architecture. This internship has given me a competitive edge in understanding cloud-native application development.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
Throughout the 12-week program, I developed technical skills in AWS core services (VPC, IAM, EC2, Lambda, S3, CloudFront, Route 53, RDS) and earned the AWS Cloud Practitioner certification. Beyond technical skills, I improved my documentation skills through blog writing, presentation skills through community sharing, and self-learning abilities through hands-on labs like CloudQuest and CloudFormation deployments.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The FCAJ community embodies the best of AWS's culture: respect, collaboration, and continuous learning. The FCAJ Community Day event showcased the strong team spirit, where participants and mentors came together to share knowledge. Being part of this community has expanded my professional network and given me a support system for my cloud learning journey.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The program provides comprehensive learning resources including AWS training materials, hands-on labs, and certification preparation support. The flexible learning schedule allows participants to balance their academic commitments while completing the bootcamp. Having access to AWS sandbox accounts for hands-on practice is invaluable for practical skill development.

### What I Found Most Satisfying

- **Hands-on Learning**: The combination of theoretical learning with practical labs and CloudQuest gamified learning made complex concepts easy to understand
- **Community Support**: The supportive community where peers and mentors actively engage and help each other
- **Real-world Projects**: The opportunity to work on actual cloud infrastructure deployments rather than just theoretical exercises
- **Career Guidance**: Mentors sharing their industry experience and helping participants understand career paths in cloud computing

### Areas for Improvement

- **More Interactive Workshops**: Additional live coding sessions or code-along workshops would enhance hands-on learning
- **Networking Events**: More frequent virtual or in-person meetups to strengthen connections within the community
- **Project-based Learning**: Opportunities to work on small team projects to apply multiple AWS services together
- **Advanced Track**: A follow-up program for participants who complete the foundation course and want to pursue specialized paths (e.g., Solutions Architect, Developer)

### Would I Recommend This Program?

**Absolutely Yes!** I would strongly recommend the First Cloud AI Journey program to any student interested in cloud computing. Here's why:

1. **Quality Education**: The curriculum is well-structured and covers essential AWS services with practical applications
2. **Supportive Environment**: The mentors and community provide excellent support throughout the learning journey
3. **Career Value**: AWS certifications and cloud skills are highly valued in the job market
4. **Free Access**: All learning resources and certification exams are provided at no cost to participants
5. **Networking**: Being part of the FCAJ community opens doors to industry connections and future opportunities

This internship has been a transformative experience that has equipped me with valuable cloud computing skills and opened my eyes to the possibilities in the cloud industry.
